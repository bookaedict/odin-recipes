Peanut Butter and Jelly

Nothing compliments a classy Italian dinner like peanut butter and jelly.

Ingredients

  • Paeanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • Bread

Directions

  1. Take out two pieces of bread
  2. Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread and jelly on the other slice of bread
  3. Put two pieces of bread together with filling facing the inside
